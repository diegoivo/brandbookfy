Brand Guidelines

Torrefazione Nera

Artisan Italian coffee roasted with tradition, delivered with intention. A brand identity forged in espresso and warmth.

01 — Archetype

Brand Personality

The archetype drives every visual and verbal decision — from the warmth of the palette to the weight of the wordmark.

The Lover × Creator

Passionate Sophisticated Artisanal Sensorial

Torrefazione Nera speaks as a devoted craftsperson who has spent decades perfecting a single art. The brand draws you in through the senses — the rich aroma of freshly ground beans, the dark sheen of a perfect crema, the warmth of a ceramic cup held between both hands. Every detail is deliberate, every experience an invitation to slow down and savor.

Voice Guidelines

Tone
Warm, intimate, and knowledgeable — as if guiding a guest through a private tasting
Language
Sensory-rich, precise, occasionally poetic — never clinical or hurried
Cadence
Measured and unhurried, with the confidence of someone who trusts their craft
Avoid
Slang, hyperbole, aggressive sales language, trendy jargon, exclamation marks

02 — Logo & Wordmark

Wordmark System

The wordmark pairs the italic display serif “Torrefazione” with the heavy-weight “Nera” anchor. The contrast between flowing italic and planted bold creates the brand's visual tension — tradition meeting presence.

Torrefazione Nera
Torrefazione Nera
Torrefazione Nera

Clear Space

Minimum clear space equals the cap-height of the “N” in Nera. This padding ensures the wordmark breathes on any surface.

Cap-height = minimum padding Torrefazione Nera

Usage Rules

Do
Torrefazione Nera

Use the full wordmark with the italic/bold contrast intact.

Do
Nera

Use the standalone “Nera” mark as a shorthand in constrained spaces.

Do
Torrefazione Nera

Adjust “Nera” to the lighter primary tint on dark backgrounds for legibility.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never stretch or distort the wordmark. Maintain original proportions.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never apply gradients to the wordmark. The logo must use flat, solid colors.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never set the wordmark in a substitute font. Only Playfair Display is approved.

06 — Don’t

Negative Rules

These are explicit violations of the brand system. Each one undermines the identity that Torrefazione Nera has built.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never stretch or distort the wordmark. It must always appear at its original aspect ratio.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never apply gradients to the logo. The wordmark must always use flat, solid color.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never substitute the typeface. The wordmark must always use Playfair Display.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera reserves the right to roast only the finest beans from sustainable farms worldwide.

Never set body text below 14px (0.875rem). Readability cannot be compromised for aesthetics.

Don’t
Torrefazione Nera

Never use blue or cold tones on the wordmark. The brand palette is warm — espresso, copper, cream.

Don’t
outline: none — no focus ring

Never remove focus indicators from interactive elements. Accessibility is not optional.
