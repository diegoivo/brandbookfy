Brand Guidelines

Maison.

Luxury real estate for the world's most discerning buyers. The definitive destination for properties of distinction, curated for ultra-high-net-worth individuals who accept nothing less than the exceptional.

01 — Archetype

Brand Personality

The archetype defines the emotional register, voice, and visual direction of Maison. across every touchpoint.

The Ruler with Lover Influence

Authoritative Commanding Refined Sensual

Maison. speaks from a position of established authority. The Ruler drives the structured, confident foundation -- symmetrical grids, luxurious spacing, and a restrained palette anchored in deep charcoal and gold. The Lover adds warmth and sensory richness: high-contrast serif typography, warm ivory surfaces, and a tactile quality that invites the eye to linger. Together they produce a brand that commands respect while rewarding attention to detail.

Voice Guidelines

Tone

Measured and assured. Every sentence carries weight. Maison. never raises its voice because it never needs to.

Vocabulary

Precise, elevated, never ornate. Prefer "residence" over "home," "curated" over "selected," "distinction" over "luxury." Avoid superlatives -- the properties speak for themselves.

Rhythm

Short declarative sentences punctuated by considered pauses. Let whitespace do the work. A single well-placed period carries more authority than a paragraph of persuasion.

Audience Register

Speak to equals, not aspirants. The reader already has means; they are evaluating whether Maison. meets their standard. Respect their intelligence and time.
