NutriPlan

Meal planning for busy moms who want nourishing, stress-free family meals without the overwhelm.

Feed your family with confidence.

01 — Archetype

Brand Personality

The archetype blend defines NutriPlan's emotional tone, communication style, and visual direction across every touchpoint.

The Caregiver + Sage

Nurturing Trustworthy Informed Generous

NutriPlan combines the Caregiver's warmth and protective instinct with the Sage's quiet authority. It never lectures or overwhelms. Instead, it anticipates needs, removes friction, and offers guidance that feels like advice from a knowledgeable friend who genuinely cares about your family's wellbeing.

Voice Guidelines

Warm but not saccharine

Speak with genuine care. Avoid baby talk, excessive exclamation marks, or condescending language.

Knowledgeable but not clinical

Share nutritional insight without sounding like a textbook. Use plain language rooted in evidence.

Encouraging but realistic

Celebrate small wins without toxic positivity. Acknowledge that meal planning is genuinely hard.

Concise but not cold

Respect the user's time. Every word should earn its place, but never at the expense of warmth.

02 — Logo & Wordmark

Wordmark System

The NutriPlan wordmark is a typographic mark set in Figtree 800. The identity element is the dot on the "i" in "Plan," replaced with a subtle leaf shape rendered in the warm accent color. This gives the mark its organic character without relying on a separate icon.
