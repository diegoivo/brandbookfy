Brand Guidelines

re:thread

A sustainable fashion marketplace where pre-owned designer clothing finds its next chapter. Honest, earthy, modern.

01 — Archetype

Brand Personality

The archetype defines the emotional tone, voice, and visual direction of the brand. re:thread blends the Explorer's independence with the Everyperson's warmth.

The Explorer + Everyperson

Independent Relatable Honest Pioneering

re:thread speaks to people who chart their own course through fashion. The Explorer archetype drives the brand's adventurous spirit and willingness to challenge how we consume clothing. The Everyperson influence keeps it grounded, ensuring the message never feels exclusive or preachy. Every garment has a story worth continuing, and re:thread makes that continuation feel natural rather than noble.

Voice Guidelines

Tone
Warm and direct. Like a friend who happens to have great taste, not a lecturer.
Language
Clear and concrete. Avoid jargon about sustainability. Say what you mean in everyday words.
Perspective
We are alongside the customer, not above them. Discovery is shared, not prescribed.
Avoid
Guilt-driven messaging, virtue signaling, greenwashing buzzwords, and anything that sounds like a corporate sustainability report.

02 — Logo & Wordmark

Wordmark System

The wordmark uses the "re:" prefix as the identity element. The colon creates a natural visual break that signals recycling, renewal, and re-discovery. The prefix carries the brand's primary color while the rest of the name stays in the text color.

06 — Don't

Negative Rules

These are explicit violations of the re:thread brand system. Each rule protects the brand's integrity and prevents the visual identity from drifting.

Don't
re:thread

Never stretch or distort the wordmark. Always use it at its original proportions.

Don't
re:thread

Never apply gradients to the wordmark. The logo must always use flat, solid colors.

Don't
re:thread

Never use unapproved fonts for the wordmark. It must always use Outfit.

Don't
This text is too small to read comfortably on screens.

Never set body text below 14px (0.875rem). Readability is non-negotiable.

Don't
re:thread

Never rotate or angle the wordmark. It should always sit on a horizontal baseline.

Don't
re: thread

Never use unapproved color combinations. Neon or high-saturation colors undermine the earthy tone.

Don't

Never remove focus indicators from interactive elements. Accessibility is mandatory.

Don't
re:thread

Never use the brand fonts for purely decorative display at extreme sizes. The wordmark has defined sizes.
